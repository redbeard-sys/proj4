DEFCON

Defcon is the biggest hacking conference in the world. Held every August in Las Vegas; it is a can't miss experience for cyber security perfessionals and enthusiests. Defcon was started in 1993 by Jeff Moss aka Dark Tangent, as a small conference for people who frequented hacker bullitin boards. After working in the information security field for many years, he was appointed to the Homeland Security Advisory Council of the Obama administration. Every year about 20,000 people attend the conference! The demographic of attendies is diverse. Regular attendies include but not limited to: IT professionals, programmers, government contractors, politicians and even lawyers. The conference holds competitions in cryptography, hacking, and social engineering. Want to learn how to hack: cars, voting machines, sex toys and more. This is the conference for you. If you chose to attend, use an alias in order to maintain anonymity.
